Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026  ·  babajanmart.com

🔒

Your Privacy Matters

At BABA JAAN MART, we believe privacy is a right, not a feature. This policy explains clearly what information we collect, why we need it, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep it simple because we have nothing to hide.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party — ever. Full stop.

📋

Information We Collect

We only collect what is strictly necessary to process and deliver your order:

  • Full Name — to address your order and delivery documents
  • Phone Number — to contact you about your order status or delivery
  • Delivery Address, City & Pincode — to ship your order to the right place
  • Order Details — items purchased, quantities, sizes, and preferences
  • Payment Status — whether payment was successful (not card or UPI credentials)

We do not collect unnecessary information such as your date of birth, gender identity, or browsing history beyond what is needed for your shopping session.

💳

Payment Security

All payments on BABA JAAN MART are processed securely through Razorpay, a leading Indian payment gateway that is PCI-DSS compliant.

  • We never see or store your card numbers, CVV, UPI PIN, or net banking credentials
  • All transaction data is encrypted end-to-end by Razorpay's secure servers
  • We only receive a confirmation of successful or failed payment status

Your financial credentials are handled exclusively by Razorpay. BABA JAAN MART has zero access to your payment instrument details.

⚙️

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes only:

  • Processing and confirming your order
  • Arranging delivery and sharing tracking updates when available
  • Contacting you for order-related support or clarifications
  • Sending order confirmation messages via WhatsApp or SMS
  • Handling return, exchange, or refund requests if raised

We may occasionally reach out to inform you about new arrivals or exclusive offers — but only through channels you have used to contact us. You can opt out at any time by messaging us.

🚫

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Your personal data is never sold, rented, exchanged, or disclosed to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. We share your delivery details only with the logistics/courier partner responsible for delivering your order — and only to the extent required for delivery.

🍪

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use standard browser cookies and Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our pages. This data is aggregated and anonymised — it does not personally identify you.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. This may affect some website functionality.

🗃️

Data Retention

We retain your order and contact information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil orders, handle returns, and meet any legal or accounting obligations. If you wish to have your data deleted after your order is complete, you may contact us and we will process the request promptly.

✅

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to any legal obligations)
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at babajanmart4366@gmail.com.

👶

Children's Privacy

BABA JAAN MART is designed for adult shoppers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us their information, please contact us and we will delete it.

📬

Contact Us

For any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, reach out to us:

  • Email: babajanmart4366@gmail.com
  • Website: babajanmart.com

We are committed to responding to all privacy-related queries within 3 business days.

Shipping Policy

Effective Date: June 2026  ·  babajanmart.com

01

Place Order

You place your order on our website and payment is confirmed via Razorpay.

02

Manual Processing

Our team reviews and packs your order with care before handing it to a courier.

03

Delivered to You

Your order is shipped and tracking details are shared with you when available.

📦

Order Processing

All orders placed on BABA JAAN MART are processed manually by our team. This means each order receives personal attention to ensure quality and accuracy before dispatch.

Because we handle orders manually, processing times may vary slightly depending on order volume and product availability. We appreciate your patience — it ensures you receive your order right.

Once your order is confirmed and payment is verified, we begin preparing it for dispatch. You will be notified via the contact details provided at checkout when your order is on its way.

🗓️

Delivery Timelines

Estimated — May Vary

Delivery timelines depend on your location and the courier partner assigned to your area. Below are general estimates:

Same City / Local

Typically 1–3 business days after dispatch

Within the State

Typically 2–4 business days after dispatch

Pan India (Other States)

Typically 4–7 business days after dispatch

Remote / Rural Locations

May take 7–10 business days; timelines vary by pincode serviceability

These are estimates only. Actual delivery times may be affected by public holidays, courier load, weather conditions, or other factors outside our control.

💰

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges may vary depending on the following factors:

  • Your delivery location (city, state, or pincode)
  • The total weight or size of your order
  • The courier service available in your area
  • Any special handling requirements for your items

Applicable shipping charges, if any, will be clearly displayed at checkout before you confirm and pay for your order. We do not add hidden charges after order placement.

📍

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, we will share tracking information with you via WhatsApp or SMS on the phone number provided at checkout — when tracking is available from the assigned courier partner.

Please note: not all courier services used by BABA JAAN MART provide real-time tracking for every location. If tracking details are not available, we will still keep you informed about your order status directly.

If you have not received any update within the expected delivery window, please reach out to us at babajanmart4366@gmail.com and we will investigate immediately.

🛡️

No Fake Delivery Guarantee

We take delivery integrity seriously. BABA JAAN MART does not engage in or tolerate fake delivery attempts — where a courier marks an order as delivered without actually attempting delivery.

  • If you receive a "delivered" status but did not receive your package, contact us within 24 hours
  • We will raise a complaint with the courier partner and investigate the matter
  • A resolution — re-delivery or refund — will be offered based on the investigation outcome

Please ensure the phone number provided at checkout is reachable so the delivery partner can contact you before arriving.

🔄

Undelivered or Returned Orders

If a delivery attempt fails due to an incorrect address, unreachable phone number, or customer unavailability, the courier may return the package to us. In such cases:

  • We will contact you to confirm your address and arrange re-shipment
  • Re-shipping charges may apply for orders returned due to customer-side issues
  • If you choose not to re-receive the order, a refund may be issued after deducting shipping costs
📬

Shipping Queries

For any questions about your shipment, delivery status, or shipping charges, contact our team directly:

  • Email: babajanmart4366@gmail.com
  • Website: babajanmart.com

We aim to respond to all shipping queries within 1 business day.

Our Return Philosophy

At BABA JAAN MART, we stand behind the quality of every product we ship. If something is not right, we want to make it right. This policy outlines exactly when and how you can raise a return or exchange request, so there are no surprises.

Please read this policy carefully before placing an order. By completing a purchase on babajanmart.com, you agree to the terms outlined here.

📅

Return Window & Order Cancellation

Return / Exchange Requests: You may request a return or exchange within 4 days of delivery. Requests raised after this window will not be eligible for return or exchange under any circumstances.

Order Cancellation: You may cancel your order at any time before it is dispatched. Once an order has been dispatched and handed to the courier, it cannot be cancelled. Please contact us immediately at babajanmart4366@gmail.com if you wish to cancel.

✅

Return Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible for a return or exchange, all of the following conditions must be met:

  • The return request is raised within 4 days of delivery
  • The product is unused, unworn, and unwashed
  • All original tags are intact and attached to the product
  • The product is in its original packaging with no damage caused by the customer
  • A valid reason is provided along with the request

✓ Eligible for Return

  • Wrong item delivered by us
  • Defective or damaged product
  • Item significantly different from description
  • Manufacturing defect

✕ Not Eligible for Return

  • Used, worn, or washed items
  • Tags removed or packaging damaged
  • Perfumes & personal care products
  • Request raised after 4 days of delivery
👤

Change of Mind & Size Issues

We understand that shopping decisions can change. However, if a return or exchange is requested due to any of the following reasons, return and re-shipping charges will be borne by the customer:

  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Wrong size selected by the customer at the time of ordering
  • Personal preference or dislike after receiving the product
  • Product matching the description but not meeting personal expectations

We strongly recommend checking our size guide carefully before placing your order. If you are unsure about sizing, contact us before purchasing and we will help you choose the right fit.

⚠️

Wrong, Damaged, or Defective Product

If you receive a product that is wrong, damaged, or defective, we sincerely apologise and will resolve it promptly. Please follow these steps:

⏱ You must report wrong, damaged, or defective products within 48 hours of delivery. Reports raised after 48 hours will not be accepted.

  • Contact us at babajanmart4366@gmail.com within 48 hours
  • Provide your order number and a clear description of the issue
  • Attach clear photos and/or videos showing the defect, damage, or wrong item
  • Our team will review your submission within 1–2 business days
  • If the claim is verified, we will arrange a free return pickup or exchange at no charge to you

Claims submitted without photo/video evidence may not be processed. Please ensure your evidence clearly shows the issue.

🚫

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following categories are strictly non-returnable and non-exchangeable under any circumstances:

  • Perfumes and fragrances
  • Personal care products (grooming, skincare, etc.)
  • Any product that has been used, opened, or had its seal broken

Please review your order carefully before confirming purchase of these items.

💸

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected by our team, we will process your refund. Here is how it works:

1

Raise Return Request

Contact us within 4 days of delivery with your order details and reason.

2

Return the Product

Ship the product back unused, with original tags and packaging intact.

3

Product Inspection

Our team inspects the returned item to verify eligibility within 1–2 business days.

4

Refund Issued

Refund is processed to your original payment method. This may take 5–7 business days to reflect.

Refund timelines (5–7 business days) depend on your bank or payment provider after we initiate the transfer. We have no control over bank processing times once the refund is dispatched from our end.

📬

Initiate a Return or Exchange

To raise a return, exchange, or refund request, contact our team directly. Please have your order number and supporting photos/videos ready:

  • Email: babajanmart4366@gmail.com
  • Website: babajanmart.com

We aim to respond to all return requests within 1–2 business days. Your satisfaction is our priority — we will always work with you to find a fair resolution.
